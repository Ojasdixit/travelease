Important Info

Company Policies

Transparency is our core value. Please review our policies below.

Booking Policy

  • All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation by third-party providers.
  • Customers must provide accurate legal names matching official travel documents.
  • Prices are indicative until fully confirmed and paid; subject to fare fluctuations.
  • Travelease reserves the right to cancel if payment is not received on time.
  • Customers are responsible for passports, visas, and health regulations.

Cancellation Policy

  • Requests must be submitted in writing via email or official channels.
  • Charges vary based on airline/hotel policies. Some bookings are non-refundable.
  • Travelease service fees are non-refundable once booking is initiated.
  • Force majeure refunds are subject to supplier policies.
  • Recommendation: Purchase travel insurance for coverage.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are processed per supplier rules (Avg. timeline: 7-30 business days).
  • Refunds are returned to the original payment method.
  • Travelease is not liable for bank or gateway delays.
  • Service fees and taxes may be deducted from the refund amount.

Privacy & Data Protection

  • We collect data solely for providing travel services and legal obligations.
  • We do not sell or rent customer data.
  • Data is shared with providers (airlines, hotels) only as required.
  • We employ reasonable security measures to safeguard your information.
